Who can complete this form?

This form is to be completed and submitted by an Eligible Physician (the "Practitioner").

What can the form be used for?

This form may be used to add, change details of, or cancel the Practitioner's attachment to a Medical Services Plan Facility Number for payment of the Business Cost Premium on Eligible Fees.

Eligible Physicians must complete and submit this form to make changes to the effective date of attachments or cancellations previously submitted.

Complete and submit separate copies of this form if you:

  • Intend to cancel their attachment to a Medical Services Plan Facility Number and add an attachment to a new Medical Services Plan Facility Number.
  • Attach to more than one Medical Services Plan Facility Number concurrently, if you qualify at each facility as an Eligible Practitioner.
